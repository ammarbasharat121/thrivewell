Publications

Listing of research publications supported by ThriveWell.

One of the important ways that ThriveWell serves as the world’s leading men’s mental health resource is through its commitment to research.

RESEARCH HIGHLIGHTS

Men’s experience of ThriveWell
  • This study surveyed 443 men who visited ThriveWell to assess the site’s impact on mental health literacy, stigma reduction, attitudes, action-taking, help-seeking, and overall satisfaction with our resource.
MEN’S MENTAL HEALTH IN THE WORKPLACE

In partnership with B.C.’s Community Savings Credit Union, this study surveyed 451 Canadian men about their mental health and how they felt about their places of work.

  • Media mentions:
    • UBC-led study finds almost half of Canadian men at risk for depression | Vancouver Sun
