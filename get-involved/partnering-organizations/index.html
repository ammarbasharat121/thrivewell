ThriveWell EXTENDS HEARTFELT GRATITUDE TO ALL OUR GENEROUS DONORS AND PARTNERS

ThriveWell survives and thrives on the invaluable support of our partners, whose dedication fuels our mission to champion men’s mental health.

Current funding partners include:

 

VISIONARY PARTNERS


King of Floors and its owners The Newell Family have been critical partners and pivotal allies of ThriveWell since 2018. Their timely support ensured the survival of ThriveWell and continues to contribute to our growth. 

PIONEERING PARTNERS

The Sip Around the World Fundraisers, spearheaded by Darius Eghdami and Raymond Dich, have quickly earned a reputation of being the “go-to” events of the year and largest fundraising events for ThriveWell. 

Learn more about and see photos and videos from Sip Around the World events.

After funding the successful launch of the Self-Check Suite in 2024, West Fraser has committed to supporting its expansion in 2025 and is also funding the development of a new Self-Guided Peer Support Course to help people recognize signs of mental health distress, build supportive relationships, and connect others to appropriate resources—furthering our shared mission to support men’s health and well-being.
