OUR COMMUNITY PARTNERS

TOGETHER, WE CAN MAKE A BIGGER IMPACT

“I was very reassured the first time I found this site. I was very alone and scared … This site felt like it spoke to me more personally.” Website visitor

Join us in changing the way we talk about and approach men’s mental health

We are honoured to collaborate with organizations and individuals around the world who share our passion for mental health advocacy and community support. Together, our collective efforts aim to catalyze positive transformation and promote well-being within our communities.

Our community partners play a vital role in our mission, offering support in diverse ways. This includes sharing our social media content, linking to our resources, providing discounted or in-kind services, as well as fundraising efforts. 

Collaborations and Promotions

In our ongoing collaborations and promotions, we partner with a diverse range of organizations, including research institutions, other non-governmental organizations (NGOs), and alumni sports associations, all of which share our commitment to supporting men’s mental health. 

Through these partnerships, we strive to expand our reach, promote awareness, and deliver valuable resources to men and their communities.

In-Kind Support

Our community partners generously provide ‘in kind’ support, offering valuable services, resources, or expertise that directly contribute to our mission and initiatives. This support enhances our ability to reach and assist individuals in need within our communities.

PrintPrint Vancouver provides ThriveWell with heavily discounted printing prices, allowing us to share our promotional cards, brochures, and other print material with our global audience.

Rogers Sports & Media supports ThriveWell through in-kind Public Service Announcements (PSA) to help raise awareness of our life-saving resources to audiences across Canada.

To explore community partnership opportunities with ThriveWell, please connect with us at info@thrivewell.org and share your ideas. We look forward to hearing from you! 

Reach Out To Become a Community Partner

If you or an organization you represent are able to support ThriveWell through financial contributions, please see our Become a Partner page for more information. Meet our Funding Partners, who provide essential financial support.
