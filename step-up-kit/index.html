Video Script

Grab your phone, film yourself from the waist up (ideally outside), and share:

1. Your name

2. Who you’re stepping up for 

(Dad, brother, partner, son, uncle, friend, co-worker, nephew, etc.)

3. What you’re doing or why this matters to you

4. Close with: “You are not alone, I’m here for you.”

Your message can be personal, light, heartfelt, or bold — whatever feels right to you. 

Here are a few examples: 

  • Hi, I‘m Frank and I’m stepping up for my brother, who I lost to suicide. To all the other guys out there who might be struggling, I want you to know that you’re not alone, I’m here for you. 
  • Hi, I’m David and I’m stepping up for my best friend by taking him on a hike, because I know what depression feels like. No one should go through that alone. You are not alone, I’m here for you. 
  • Hi, I’m Linda, and I’m stepping up and checking in with the guys in my life to let them know that they’re not alone, I’m here for them. 
  • Hi, I’m Grant and I’m stepping up for my son so he knows it’s strong to talk about mental health. You are not alone, I’m here for you. 

It’s simple. It’s personal. It’s powerful. 

And your voice could be the one that helps someone else step up too.
